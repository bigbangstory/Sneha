The Curriculum · Two Signature Looks

A day and a night, mastered.

Look One

11:00 AM – 12:30 PM

Day Bridal

The Day Bridal look is centred around fresh, radiant skin and soft, refined beauty that enhances the bride's natural features.

Learn how to create a luminous complexion, seamless blending, delicate eye definition, naturally sculpted brows and balanced tones that come together in a timeless, elegant finish designed to last beautifully throughout the day.

Look Two

1:00 PM – 2:30 PM

Night Bridal

The Night Bridal look focuses on creating a polished, elevated glam with greater depth, definition and dimension.

Explore techniques for building a flawless complexion, creating statement eyes, enhancing facial structure and selecting richer tones that photograph beautifully under evening lighting, while maintaining a sophisticated, long-lasting finish.
